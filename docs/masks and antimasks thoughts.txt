mask 0 enabled: stencil & 0001 must = 0001
"anything inside the mask is drawn"

antimask 0 enabled: aka stencil & 0001 must = 0
"anything outside the mask is drawn"

mask 0 mask 1 enabled: stencil & 0011 must equal 0011
"anything inside both masks is drawn"

antimask 0 enabled antimask 1 enabled: stencil & 0011 must equal 0000
"anything outside both masks is drawn"

antimask 0 enabled mask 1 enabled: stencil & 0011 must equal 0010
"anything outside mask 0 and inside mask 1 is drawn"

mask x must either be normal or an antimask, not both
